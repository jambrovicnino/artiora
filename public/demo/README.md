This directory contains SVG placeholder images that are currently being used. The website is fully functional with these placeholders.

## To Replace with Your Photos:

## Recommended Image Specifications:

### Before/After Slider Images:
- **family-bw.jpg & family-color.jpg**:
  - Aspect ratio: 16:10 (e.g., 1600x1000px)
  - Format: JPG or PNG
  - Should be the same photo, one in B&W and one colorized
  - Resolution: At least 1200px wide for quality

### Service Card Images:
- **All three service card images**:
  - Aspect ratio: 4:5 (portrait, e.g., 800x1000px)
  - Format: JPG or PNG
  - Should showcase different restoration styles
  - Resolution: At least 600px wide for quality

## Note:

The SVG placeholders are designed to match the vintage aesthetic and show clearly what type of image belongs there. You can keep them or replace them with actual photos at any time!
